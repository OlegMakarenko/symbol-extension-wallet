Privacy Policy for Symbol Wallet

Effective Date: May 17, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Symbol Wallet. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how Symbol Wallet collects, uses, and protects your information.

How Do You Accept This Policy

By using Symbol Wallet, including downloading one of our mobile applications or web-browser extension, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. Please review it carefully and ensure you understand how your data is managed and protected.

IF YOU DO NOT AGREE WITH ANY PART OF THIS PRIVACY POLICY OR OUR TERMS OF USE, THEN PLEASE DO NOT USE ANY OF THE SERVICES.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Data Stored Locally: Symbol Wallet stores sensitive personal data, including your mnemonic passphrase and account keys, locally on your device using the browser's local storage. This information is encrypted to enhance security.
  • Automatic Data Collection: When you use Symbol Wallet, third-party services may automatically receive certain information through request headers, such as your IP address and user agent string. This data can potentially be used to track and identify you, although Symbol Wallet does not have control over this process.

Data Usage and Sharing

  • Local Storage: Your mnemonic passphrase and private keys are stored in encrypted form in the browser's local storage on your device. This information is not shared with any third parties.
  • Third-Party Information Access: Some personal information, such as your IP address and user agent string, is automatically sent to third-party services due to the nature of internet communications. Symbol Wallet does not have control over this data exchange.
  • Blockchain Transactions: Symbol Wallet uses the Symbol Blockchain to process transactions. Information such as addresses, transaction amounts, and other details are publicly visible on the blockchain and can be accessed by anyone.

Security Measures

While we take measures to encrypt your sensitive data and store it securely, it is important to note that:

  • Risk of Compromise: Encrypted data stored in your browser's local storage can be compromised by malicious software on your device, potentially leading to the loss of all your funds.
  • Recommended Use: Symbol Wallet is not recommended for storing large amounts of funds. Use it cautiously and ensure your device is secure.

Data Recovery and Responsibility

  • No Data Recovery: Symbol Wallet does not store your mnemonic passphrase or private keys on any external servers. Therefore, it is impossible to recover access to your account if you lose your mnemonic passphrase.
  • User Responsibility: Users are solely responsible for securely storing their mnemonic passphrase and private keys.

Public Data on Blockchain

  • Visibility: All transactions made using Symbol Wallet are recorded on the Symbol Blockchain. Information such as addresses, transaction amounts, and other transaction details are publicly accessible and can be viewed by anyone.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
